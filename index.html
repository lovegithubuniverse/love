Dear Simar

Your Secret Admirer
September 17, 2021
Love Care

The first time I saw you, I never thought it would come to this and that I would be writing a letter to you in such an emotional state. It was a good time and now I feel lucky to have met you, such an amazing person. I still remember the dance you did and believe me it was really good. It was very bold of you to find my ID and message me on Instagram but, I am glad you did.

I wasn't sure how should I proceed so I thought to keep it light and went anyway with the flow. To be honest, I really liked talking to you. There was always a certain confident and a happy vibe coming from you that I didn't find anywhere. Talking to you was like a dose of energy drink that would lift my energy levels up. Talking to you was good.

Moving on, these text conversations turned into phone calls. I liked how we talked to each other scared of our families but did anyway. Talking to you, I felt a certain warmness inside. I wasn't able to hide my smiles and laughter even though we were far away from each other. The connection was special, amazing and I dare say, destiny.

Life Changed

I never knew how joyous life could be until I got you as a best friend. My heart fills with joy everytime I talk or think about you. There's too much excitement that sometimes it's uncontrollable.

Even though you might deny it, and say we are just best friends, I always saw love in your conversations, your texts, your guidance and everything else. I just thought it wasn't the right time yet to take things forward and hence said nothing, enjoying the beautiful time together. But I always thought that you felt the same way as I did.

Knowing Each Other

Trust me when I say you are amazing and that I have never ever found a girl as awesome as you. You are one of a kind. I never imagined that I would truly find a person who I would want to spend so much time with, who could truly touch my heart the way you have without even trying. This is not just talk, it comes from the heart. The more I kept knowing you, the more I kept falling for you. I always felt that you were just a second version of me.

Things Changed

Though everything was very good and we both were happy, It was shortlived. I don't know what happened, but things changed and I felt you changed. Was it because of your family, was it because of examination stress, I don't know you started behaving weirdly and when I tried to confront you, It was a lot of times that we'd end up fighting. I hated fighting with you. I tried to resolve your problem which I never found.

My Feelings

Whatever the case would be, you continue to stay in my life and in my heart. You will stay there forever. It's too much for me to let you walk away. Though I know, that you now know that I am serious for you, but I never expressed my feelings for you. I don't want to live my life with the regret of not expressing my feeling for my beloved. So, I'll do it now.

I don't know when and how I fell for you deeply, but It is now what it is. I love you. I feel love in your voice when we talk. I feel love in your texts. Everyday I think about you I feel love and inspiration. If I was spelling out my favourite person right now it'd be S-I-M-A-R. Even though you might repeatedly say, I am not beautiful you'll find a lot better girl than me, believe me sometimes I can't stop looking at your photo, you look so damn good. I still remember each and everything of how you were dressed in the wedding. You looked gorgeous. I feel such powerful adoration for you and only you.

The Past

You might have loved someone in the past or not. You might have been in a relationship in the past or not. Someone might have loved you or not. I really don't care at all. I again say I don't care at all about your past. Your past is your business. Your future is my privilege. I'll accept the way you are without ever judging you.

The Future

Yes I know there's a lot of things a girl has to consider before coming into a relationship with a boy. Family, friends, what would the society say? bla bla bla. But, believe me you were really brave the day you messaged me and I have faith in you that you are really brave even today. You are one of the strongest people I know. Just open your heart, I know there is society pressure but leave it. Anyways, you are migrating to Canada, no one cares then.

Though I cannot force you for anything, still I would suggest you to listen to your heart. Don't worry too much about the future now. The future will be great. I don't know what will happen in the future. But, I know that in my present, I want to be with you. Enjoy the peaceful loving moments with you. I am not boasting, but it's true that you might never ever find a guy like me. So, please think about it once.

Ending Note

I love you truly, madly & deeply. I never felt the same for someone else. You enlighten my heart and my world. I can't believe how hard I've fallen for you. You are my treasure – the most precious thing in my life.

To you – the only person I will ever love.

Will you be mine?
